# 🎮 Perkmaster's Dungeon

A level-based 2D action-adventure game built from scratch using **Processing (Java)**, featuring a dynamic progression and "Perk" crafting system.

## 📖 Story & Overview
In a world where the mythical "Perk Monsters" who once protected the realm have been wiped out by an evil force, only one remains. You play as a wandering magician tasked with helping this last Perkmaster. By defeating enemies and exploring levels, you collect **Soul Flowers** used to craft powerful Perks that grant you unique enhancements and abilities.

---

## 🛠️ Key Features & Mechanics

* **Dynamic Perk Shop System:** Spend collected Soul Flowers to unlock and craft various perks. You have 3 active perk slots available to customize your build. Perks feature color-coded rarity tiers (e.g., Legendary).
  * *Helping Hand:* Enables passive health regeneration.
  * *Companion:* Increases your strength dynamically based on the number of enemies currently on the level.
  * *Bookworm:* Accelerates your mana regeneration rate.
  * *Adrenaline:* Grants a major speed boost and a visual motion trail when health drops below 50%.
  * *Fish Ancestors:* Allows infinite underwater breathing and regenerates health while submerged.
* **Oxygen Gauge / Water System:** Swimming underwater caps your breath at 5 seconds. Staying under longer results in drowning damage unless mitigated by specific perks.
* **Gatekeeper Progression:** To advance to the next level, you must clear out all hostile forces and defeat the designated room guardians protecting the exit portal.
* **Custom Artwork:** 90% of the game’s visual assets—including the main character animations, UI/HUD overlays, and environmental backdrops—were custom-designed for this project.

---

## ⌨️ Controls

* **W, A, D:** Movement and Jumping
* **E:** Interact / Open & Close the Perk Shop
* **Left Click:** Shoot Fireballs
